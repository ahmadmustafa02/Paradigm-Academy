About Paradigm

Paradigm Academy, founded in September 2023 by Mr. Adeel and Mr. Haris Hashmi, offers specialized education for students in grades 6 to 12. The academy focuses on academic excellence and prepares students for university entrance exams. With a commitment to high-quality teaching, Paradigm Academy aims to build a strong foundation for higher education.
